Andrew Gordon

Early Life

Born on December 19,1994 in Bardstown, Kentucky. He was the son of Logan Gordon II and Susan M. Schlegel. Andrew was one of two children. Andrew went to school at Bardstown highschool and then later went to Bellarmine Univeristy in 2013 to study Business. In 2017 he graduated, and in 2019 Andrew returned to Bellarmine for an MBA. Upon graduating with an MBA at Bellarmine University in 2021, Andrew decided to transition into a career in software engineering. Employment Journey

  1. While Andrew was going through his undergraduate studies, Andrew worked as a co-op on the Sears Sales team. In this work he helped to analyze important sell-thru values for the Sales team.
  2. After graduating in 2017, Andrew first started his work experience at Total Quality Logistics where he learned the basics of the transportation industry. This was a job that required him to cold-call prospective customers and develop his own book of business.
  3. After a short stint at TQL, Andrew went on to transition to Sales operations at GE Appliances in Louisville, KY. This position required him to work with home builders and and salespeople to figure out how many and what appliance models are needed for construction projects. Andrew has worked at GEA for three and a half years.
