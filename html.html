N'Golo Kante biography

The purpose of this biography is to document the unprecedented rise of N'Golo Kante who is a very famous soccer player. He made his professional debut by coming on in the final 11 minutes of the last game of the Ligue 2 season on the 18th of May 2012. After making his professional debut for Boulogne, he went on to play for several other football clubs. Below is a list of all the professional football clubs he's played including his current team Chelsea F.C.

  1. US Boulogne
  2. SM Caen
  3. Leicester City F.C.
  4. Chelsea F.C.

Background

N'Golo Kante was born on the 29th of March, 1991 in Paris to Malian parents. His namesake is King Ngolo Diarra of the Bamana Empire. Although many of his teammates consider him to be a shy and private individual, he has amassed a large follwing of fans and is very popular on social media. Click for links to his personal Instagram, Facebook and Twitter. Since making his professional debut at 21 years old he has went on to achieve many individual and team achievements.
